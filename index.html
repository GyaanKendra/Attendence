Employee Attendance System Guide

Step 1: Create an Account

Click on the "Sign Up" tab and fill in all your details including:

- Your full name

- Email address

- Secure password

- Username

- Mobile number

- Set your office timings and daily working hours

Step 2: Login

After creating your account, use the "Login" tab to access the system with your email and password.

Step 3: Mark Attendance

On the dashboard, you can:

- Click "Check In" when you arrive at work

- Click "Check Out" when you leave

- Mark yourself as "Absent" if you're not working that day

- Mark "Holiday" if it's a company holiday

Step 4: View Analytics

Click "View Attendance Analytics" to see detailed reports of your attendance, working hours, and overtime.

You can print or download these reports as PDF for your records.

Step 5: Profile Management

Click on the profile icon in the top right to update your personal information and settings.

Need More Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact your system administrator.
